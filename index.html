I. What I do

Staring down an assignment, essay, research paper, or thesis and not sure where to start? I've spent the last decade helping high school, college, university, and graduate students strengthen their writing, sharpen their understanding, and turn in work they're actually proud of.

I've been through both an undergraduate and a master's program myself, so I know firsthand what it's like to juggle coursework, tight deadlines, and research that feels like it's going nowhere. That's exactly why I focus on personalized support — not just fixing a paper, but helping you understand the material behind it. That might mean walking through an assignment together, shaping an essay from a rough idea into a clear argument, digging into a literature review, narrowing down a topic, building a solid outline, formatting in APA, MLA, Chicago, or Harvard style, or giving a final draft a thorough edit. For thesis and dissertation work, I can also help with research methodology and structure from start to finish.

I work across a range of fields, including nursing and healthcare, medicine and public health, English and literature, education, sociology, criminology, psychology, and business and management.

  • Original, well-researched, high-quality work
  • On-time delivery, every time
  • Personalized attention for every student

Whatever stage you're at — brainstorming, organizing research, or polishing a final draft — I'm here to help you get there.
